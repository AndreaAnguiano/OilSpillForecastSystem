The flow rates of the three main tributaries of Galveston Bay can affect the circulation and residence time of spills within the Bay. Here you can choose an estimate of the river flow or input the stage height data. The stage heights for the Trinity and San Jacinto rivers and the Buffalo Bayou must be less than or equal to 31, 28, and 38 feet respectively.
